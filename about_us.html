About us
We are a team of four based in Shanghai. Over the past three years, during the severe COVID-19 situation and frequent lockdowns in China, we have rescued and fostered approximately 100 stray cats. Additionally, we have conducted TNR (Trap-Neuter-Return) for around 70 stray cats. The success of these efforts is mainly attributed to the team's dedication.
Why us
Our team remains committed to the welfare of stray cats despite the challenges posed by the pandemic. In addition to our individual expertise, we collectively handle daily tasks such as feeding stray cats in the community, conducting TNR for them, cleaning and providing socialization training for newly acquired stray cats, caring for adopted kittens, conducting screenings for potential adopters, and facilitating the adoption process. All these routine responsibilities are shared among our team members.
